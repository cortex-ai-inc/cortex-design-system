Accent Colors

Two supporting accents exist alongside the blue signature. They are subordinate voices — decoration and categorization, never interaction. Accents never appear on buttons, links, focus rings, checked states, or active navigation; those are exclusively blue. Keep them under ~10% of any view.

Gradient

The gradient is itself a token — --gradient-primary — exposed by the .gradient-primary utility.

Preview
--gradient-primary: linear-gradient(135deg, var(--k-primary) 0%, var(--k-primary-container) 100%);
⚠️ Text on the gradient must be dark

Use text-surface-container-lowest (#0C0E14): 11.1:1 on the light end, 6.0:1 on the dark end — AA on both. Light text (on-surface) fails at 1.36:1 on the light end. This is a law, not a preference.

Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

Use surface colors to create visual hierarchy. Use k-primary for primary actions and links. Use semantic colors consistently.

✗ Don't

Use surface colors interchangeably — keep the layering consistent. Use colors outside the defined palette.
